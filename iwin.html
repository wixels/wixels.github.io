What Is IWIN?

The IWIN LMS was built from the ground up to service a need that arose in 2017. With existing LMS platforms using outdated tech stacks, we experienced limitations when trying to provide interactive learning experiences (such as VR and AR), so we decided to make our own!

IWIN stands for Information When I Need, is it a methodology developed by WinWin Consulting International, to priorities learning content that is relevant to a learner, as and when they need it, in bite sized chunks.

Key Features

The IWIN learning management system offers a wide aray of features to corporate clients, including a custom built authuring tool to develop content. In addition to the authuring tool, IWIN also supports other learning files such as xAPI and Scrom 1.4.

All user progress, assessments and tasks are tracked, with the abilities for managers and HR representative to manage user progress, reset modules and assign additional assessment attempts. All information that is tracked can be reported on and extracted, through our custom reporting tools.

Our Skillsets

Software Development.

Overview

Our development capabilities range from custom built app development, to easily manageable CMS based websites.

Our primary skills lie in the custom developed application and web application space, with JavaScript, Java, Swift, and Python being our strongest skillsets.

We have a number of developers that are utilised according to the complexity of a project, from senior developers to IT interns; in this way we are able to deliver according to the scope and budget of most development projects.

Expertise

Our primary focus is in the web development space, building progressive web applications with accessibility and scalability in mind.

Although we focus our development towards PWA's, we understand in certain scenarios native functionality is absolutely necessary. Our team can build both native iOS and Android Applications; staying away from frameworks such as Cordova / Ionic, and developing in the native Swift and Java languages for long term support and updates.

Our development expertise extend further than the web and app development space, over the years we have built a strong understanding in AR, VR, ML and AI space. In many of our projects, we have leveraged our understand and expertise in Machine Learning to improve app experiences, optimise performance and create dynamic user dashboards. In this, we have leveraged tools such as TensorFLow, Firebase and Algolia.
